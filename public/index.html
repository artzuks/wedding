Our Story

How We Met

When they first met, Art and Rebecca literally had chemistry—chemistry class that is. Way back in 2007, Art and Rebecca, gifted students that they are, were both assigned to Mr. Jacklin’s 4th period honors chemistry class. Two years later, after studying for the AP statistics exam together, Rebecca strong armed Art into going to the prom with her. The rest, as they say, is history (a class Rebecca and Art never took together).

The Proposal

At 6am on their 6th anniversary, Rebecca woke up to the sound of jingling. Afraid that the kittens, Miso and Basil, had gotten into something, she was at first comforted and then confused when Art assured her not to worry—the jingling was just from the kittens’ bow ties. Getting up, Rebecca picked up Miso and said, “This is not supposed to be on here,” referring to his bow tie. Art picked up Basil and also noted that there was something that was not supposed to be on there—a ring!
